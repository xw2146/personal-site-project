About

This is a website I designed for the Front-End-Development Class at the Flatiron School. It is a documentation of my very first attempt of a DIY project. With the help of my father, I decided to upcycle my old IKEA Billy bookcases into a built-in wall unit.
On this page you will find information on designing, planning, building and finishing a built-in bookcase, as well as the supplies, materials and tools needed. I also included some pictures for ideas and inspirations.
